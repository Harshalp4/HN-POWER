HT/LT Electrical Works

Our HT/LT division handles the complete lifecycle of high-tension and low-tension electrical systems — from EHV works at 132/33 kV substations down to LT distribution networks. From initial load studies and system design to final testing and commissioning, we deliver substations and distribution networks that power Maharashtra's largest developments.

With deep expertise in MSEDCL standards and regulations, we ensure every installation meets the highest compliance requirements. Our team manages the complete coordination with utility providers, eliminating a major headache for developers.

  • EHV/HV Substation Works — 132/33 kV Substations & 33/11 kV Lines
  • Erection of Power Transformers, MOCB, ACB & VCB
  • Substation Design & Installation (up to 33kV)
  • Transformer Supply & Commissioning
  • HT/LT Panel Boards & Switchgear
  • Cable Laying & Termination
  • Power Factor Correction Systems
  • Load Studies & System Design
  • Lighting Protection Systems

Data Center Infrastructure

In the era of digital transformation, data center reliability is non-negotiable. We design and implement power distribution systems engineered for 99.999% uptime—the 'Five 9s' standard that mission-critical facilities demand.

Our data center team understands the unique challenges of rising power density, redundancy requirements, and the critical importance of seamless failover systems. We partner with facility managers to deliver infrastructure that keeps the lights on, always.

  • Generator Integration & ATS
  • Redundant Power Path Design
  • Busbar Trunking Systems

  • Security System Projects (CCTV, Access Control)

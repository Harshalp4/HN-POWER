Our Story

H.N.Electrical — now H N Power Solutions Pvt Ltd — was established in 2004, building its foundations on trusted partnerships with HDIL builders and Lodha Group. Holding an Electrical Contractor License of the Maharashtra state, the company set out with one guiding principle: "Working for the customers – with the customers."

From our early projects in the Kalyan-Thane corridor, we earned a reputation for on-time delivery, transparent pricing, and clean, code-perfect installations. That reputation grew through consistent excellence — most of our business today comes from repeat clients and referrals, the strongest endorsement of our work.

Today, we are the trusted electrical partner for industry giants like Lodha Group and HDIL, having delivered over 500 projects across industrial, residential, infrastructure, and data center sectors. Our mission remains unchanged: build long-term client relationships by delivering superior customer value, every single project.
